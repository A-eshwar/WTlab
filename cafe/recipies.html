Our Recipe Guidelines

At Steeped Stories, every recipe is crafted with care to ensure the highest quality and consistent flavor. Our recipe standards include detailed ingredient lists, clear preparation steps, and precise serving instructions to deliver the perfect taste experience every time.

Ingredients:

  • Use fresh, high-quality ingredients with exact measurements for consistent flavor.
  • Clearly list all items including herbs, spices, and special additives.

Preparation Steps:

  • Follow step-by-step instructions, highlighting techniques like blending, simmering, or garnishing.
  • Include tips for optimal preparation to preserve flavors and textures.

Yield & Serving Size:

  • Specify the total servings produced to help with inventory and portion control.
  • Detail standard portion sizes to ensure customer satisfaction and cost management.

Cooking Time & Temperature:

  • Provide exact cooking times and temperatures to maintain consistency and food safety.
  • Highlight any critical points, such as resting times or temperature checks.

Serving & Presentation:

  • Offer plating suggestions and garnish ideas to enhance the visual appeal.
  • Include instructions on storage and reheating to maintain freshness.
